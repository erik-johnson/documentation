Automation Benefits
-------------------

#. Orchestration. Enstratius will intelligently launch and configure your servers and applications.

#. Automated backups. Enstratius will perform backup/maintenance of the application and hardware infrastructure according to the specification of the deployment.

#. Scalability. Enstratius will scale the servers to closely match infrastructure with demand.

#. Resiliency. Cloud servers by their nature are ephemeral, configuring a deployment will allow applications to survive the unexpected termination of a node.
